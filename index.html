Our Insight

Build from the curriculum, not the gradebook.

CP→ TP→ ATP→ Modul Ajar→ Assessment→ Grade→ Rapor

Grounded in Kurikulum Merdeka

Every AI generation draws from structured CP/TP data and public BSKAP PDFs — not generic knowledge. ATPs reference real learning objectives.

School-Specific Context

Pikira knows the class, the students, previous assessments. AI generates soal tuned to actual learning gaps — context ChatGPT will never have.

Compounding Data Moat

Every assessment graded feeds back into smarter suggestions. The more a school uses Pikira, the better it gets. Generic AI can't compete.

🤖 Why not just ChatGPT? → Context is the moat. It doesn't know your curriculum, students, or school.
"But the market already has LMS players."

Architecture determines destiny.

Linear vs Jira

Jira dominated PM. Linear won by building around speed — you can't make Jira fast with a plugin.

Notion vs Confluence

Notion's block model enabled features Confluence's page architecture couldn't support without a rewrite.

Canva vs Adobe

Canva built for a different user around a different workflow. Adobe couldn't cannibalize their pro tools.

Sokrates/Quintal built around the gradebook. Pikira builds around the curriculum pipeline. Our AI workflow can't exist as a Sokrates add-on — just like Linear couldn't be a Jira plugin.

Step 1 — Workflow Automation

Teacher picks a learning objective. Pikira generates the ATP, drafts the Modul Ajar, creates assessment soal, grades student work. The teacher reviews, adjusts, approves — never starts from a blank page.
